FAQ

How do I play?

Tap words from the daily bank. Cooler words are farther from the hidden target by meaning; warmer words are closer. Find the hidden word in as few picks as possible.

Does WARMER require an account?

No. WARMER works without accounts, ads, analytics, or tracking.

Where are my stats stored?

Your progress, streaks, settings, and archive scores are stored locally on your device.
