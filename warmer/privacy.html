Privacy Policy

WARMER - Daily Meaning Puzzle
Last updated: June 18, 2026

Summary: WARMER does not collect, store, or share personal data. Game data stays on your device.

Information We Do Not Collect

WARMER does not collect personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, locations, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, analytics events, or tracking data.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app may store progress, streaks, stats, settings, reminder preferences, and archive scores locally on your device. This data is not sent to us.

Network, Accounts, Ads, and Analytics

WARMER has no user accounts, no ads, no analytics SDKs, no tracking SDKs, and no runtime backend for puzzle scoring. The daily puzzle content is bundled with the app.

Notifications and Sharing

If you enable daily reminders, they are scheduled locally on your device. If you choose to share a result, your device's standard share sheet sends only the content you choose to the app you select.

Children's Privacy

WARMER does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.
